## About CEPROC

We are the Canada Export Promotion Council unifying industry advocacy, shaping trade policy, and forging high-value international market connections for Canadian business.

## Our Approach

- Trusted Authority with industry-backed expertise
- Policy-Driven and informed by trade data
- Verified Connectivity through pre-qualified partnerships
- Dedicated to Canadian Growth in every decision

## The Four Pillars

### 1. Global Advocacy

Your voice in Ottawa and on the global stage. We lobby on behalf of Canadian exporters to reduce trade barriers and safeguard fair market access.

### 2. Trade Policy & Compliance

Navigate complexity with certainty. Expert analysis on CETA, CUSMA, CPTPP, customs modernization, and compliance standards.

### 3. Verified Connectivity

We facilitate B2B introductions between verified Canadian producers and pre-qualified international buyers.

### 4. Strategic Market Making

Leveraging proprietary market intelligence to identify high-growth export regions for profitable market entry.
