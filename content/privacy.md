The Canadian Export Promotion Council (CEPROC) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with applicable Canadian privacy laws, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you:

- Register as a member or subscriber of CEPROC
- Contact us via forms, email, or social media
- Participate in CEPROC events, programs, or surveys

This information may include your name, business name, mailing address, email address, phone number, country of operation, and trade-related interests.

We may also collect non-identifiable information (e.g., browser type, device, general location) through website analytics to improve our services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

- Provide membership benefits and communications
- Manage event registrations and participation
- Share information about export opportunities, trade missions, and updates
- Improve our services and understand member needs
- Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

## 3. Consent

By providing your information, you consent to its collection, use, and disclosure in accordance with this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at [privacy@ceproc.ca](mailto:privacy@ceproc.ca), subject to legal or contractual restrictions.

## 4. Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes stated above. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data against loss, theft, or unauthorized access.

## 5. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose information:

- To service providers who assist in operating CEPROC (e.g., email, IT, analytics) under confidentiality agreements
- As required by law or to comply with legal processes
- In the event of an organizational restructuring or merger, in accordance with PIPEDA

## 6. Access and Correction

You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting [privacy@ceproc.ca](mailto:privacy@ceproc.ca).

## 7. Cookies and Analytics

CEPROC.ca may use cookies and analytics tools to improve website functionality and understand visitor interactions. You can adjust browser settings to manage cookies.
